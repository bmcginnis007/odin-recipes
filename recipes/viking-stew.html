Medieval Viking Stew

This stew has it all: Meat, vegetables, and BEER!

This delicious viking stew harkens back to a time when our food was cooked with fire. Fire that kept us warm on cold, snowy nights and protected us from unseen threats lurking in the darkness.

Ingredients

  • 2 large onions - roughly diced
  • 4 cloves garlic- crushed
  • 2 large potatoes - chopped into chunks
  • 1lb meat of choice - Beef shin, Venison, Smoked sausage
  • ½ lb bacon - chopped roughly
  • ½ cabbage - or other vegetables like carrot or celery roughly chopped or grated
  • 16 oz beer - Dark beer works best
  • 16 oz water/meat or vegetable stock

Instructions

  1. Place all the ingredients together into the pot and mix it well.
  2. Cook it at low medium temperature for about 5 hours monitoring it every now and again adding water or stock if needed.
  3. Season with salt and pepper when done.
  4. You can also chop up some herbs(parsley, marjoram, dill)and add it in when done.
